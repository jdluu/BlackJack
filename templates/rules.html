BlackJack Rules

The rules of BlackJack vary from casino to casino. However, the game generally follows hit and stand rules.
The goal of the game is to get as close to 21 as possible without going over.
Here's a quick synopsis of the rules:

  • Each player is dealt two cards face up.
  • The dealer is dealt two cards, one face up and one face down.
  • Number cards are worth their face value
  • Face cards are worth 10
  • Aces can be worth 11 or 1
  • The player can choose to hit or stand.
  • If the player chooses to hit, they are dealt another card.
  • If the player chooses to stand, they are done with their turn.
  • The dealer will hit until they have 17 or more.
  • The player with the highest hand wins.
